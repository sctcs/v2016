iChinese快速认字配套游戏

(请用Internet Explorer. 不能用Firefox)

本网站提供的游戏是iChinese快速认字的配套游戏。 我们会不停地增加新的内容。

 

请让孩子每学一课,认字差不多了, 就玩该课的游戏。 请孩子边玩边大声读字。

 

Note: Matchword is played by two persons. Play with your siblings or parents and win the game! 

Note: HOW TO MAKE THE FONT LARGER IN THE GAME. If you are using IE and the font is too small in the game window, hit key "F11" on your keyboard (or go to View-> Full Screen) which will make your game window larger. Then click "New Game" twice. To return to normal display, hit "F11" again.
