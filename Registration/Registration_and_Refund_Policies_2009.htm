SCCS 2009-2010 Registration and Refund Policies

 

 

1.      Registration Policy:

  • First school day for 2009-2010 Fall term is 9/13/2009
  • Annual registration for Fall and Spring terms during summer.
  • Change (drop, switch, add) between enrichment class for Spring is allowed before end of Fall term
  • Annual family membership (non-refundable, due on 9/13/2009): $20
  • Tuition for language class (two terms): $240
  • Tuition for enrichment class: $70 per term, $140 per year
  • Annual Registration fee:  $20 (non-refundable, due on 9/13/2009)
  • Early registration (2 weeks before the first day, on or before 8/30): registration fee waived.
  • Late registration (after the first day): $10 late registration fee will be added to $20 regular registration fee, total registration fee will be $30 (late registration fee does not apply to New Students, who needs pay $20 regular registration fee if not registered before early registration deadline)
  • Full payment of all annual fees and tuitions of both terms before Fall term starts is preferable, but class tuition can be paid with two payments, Fall and Spring payments.
  • Due date of Fall payment is the first school day: 9/13/2009
  • Due date of the Spring payment: 12/31/2009
  • $5 late payment charge is added after due date

2.      Refund Policy

  • Before 3rd class (9/27/2009): full refund
  • Cancelled class by school: full refund
  • Before the 4th class (10/04/2009): 75%
  • On or after the 4th class (10/04/2009): no refund
  • Membership fee: no refund
  • Registration Fee: no refund
